**About CodeMaster Chrome Extension**

Discover the fusion of coding challenges with the Leitner system of learning! CodeMaster is not just another Chrome extension – it's your personalized coding companion. Dive into coding exercises on platforms like LeetCode, and let CodeMaster track your performance, down to the very seconds it took to solve.

Stumbled on a problem? No worries. Every hiccup is a chance to learn. Failed attempts are instantly reviewed, prioritized, and stored for reinforcement, thanks to the ingenious Leitner methodology.

But that's not all. Open a new world of flashcards tailored from your coding journey. From data structures to algorithms that have been your Achilles' heel, revisit them in a unique blend of active recall and practice. Get ready to code on-the-go with mini code editor cards, quiz yourself with intuitive word cards, or delve deep with specialized challenges like the Dutch national flag algorithm.

Automatically generated and always adapting, CodeMaster harnesses the power of ChatGPT to craft cards that truly resonate with your learning curve.

Unlock your coding potential. Master. Reinforce. Excel.
